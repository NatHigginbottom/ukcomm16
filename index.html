About the Conference

Expect to connect with hundreds of your government colleagues and learn from the brightest minds in communications and service design. At the seventh Annual GovDelivery Public Sector Communications Conference, you’ll learn exactly how they’re helping to incite real action in communities and achieve results.

About GovDelivery

Over 1,800 public sector organisations use GovDelivery’s highly-secure cloud solutions every day to enhance the citizen experience for more than 120 million people worldwide. GovDelivery offers leading solutions for managing government communications, internal and external learning, and open data. The GovDelivery Network offers a unique and impactful way for public sector organisations to work together to cross-promote content and increase their digital reach. Organisations using GovDelivery see higher utilisation of citizen services and greater citizen engagement.
